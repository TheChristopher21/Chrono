# Chrono Android Privacy Policy Draft

This draft is for legal/product review before publishing. The public Play Console URL should point to the final hosted policy, for example:

https://chrono-logisch.ch/datenschutz

## Controller and Contact

Chrono / Chronologisch

Contact: info@chrono-logisch.ch

## Purpose

The Chrono Android app provides access to the Chrono platform for working time tracking, absence management, payroll-related workflows, scheduling, company administration and operational modules such as CRM, banking, accounting and supply chain.

## Data Processed

Depending on enabled company features and user permissions, the app may process:

- Account and profile data, including name, username, email, role and personnel number.
- Working time data, including clock-in/out entries, breaks, daily notes, balances and customer/project/task assignments.
- Absence and correction data, including vacation, sick leave and correction requests.
- Payroll-related data, including payslip metadata and approval status.
- Admin workflow data, including employee, company, schedule, accounting, CRM, banking and supply-chain records.
- Authentication data, including a session token stored locally on the device.

## Legal Basis

Final legal bases must be confirmed for the production operator and target market. Likely bases include contract performance, employment administration, legal obligations and legitimate interests in secure business operations.

## Data Transfer

The app communicates with the Chrono backend over the configured production API:

https://api.chrono-logisch.ch

Release builds do not allow cleartext HTTP traffic.

## Local Storage

The app stores the login token locally to keep the user signed in. Users can remove this data by logging out or clearing app data on the device.

## Third-Party SDKs

The current native Android app does not include advertising or analytics SDKs.

## Retention and Deletion

Retention depends on company configuration, legal obligations and the Chrono backend. Users can request information or deletion via:

info@chrono-logisch.ch

## User Rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion or restriction of their data where applicable under relevant privacy laws.

## Changes

This policy should be updated whenever app features, backend processors, retention rules or data categories change.
