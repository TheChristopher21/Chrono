# Chrono

## App Name

Chrono

## Short Description

Time tracking, absences, payroll and business workflows in one app

## Full Description

Chrono is the native Android app for teams that manage working time, absences, payroll documents and operational workflows in one connected system.

Employees can clock in and out, review daily and weekly summaries, add daily notes and assign time to customers, projects and tasks. Vacation requests, sick leave and time correction requests can be submitted directly from the app.

Admins get native views for team status, time accounts, scheduling, payroll, accounting, CRM, banking, company settings and supply chain. Visible areas depend on roles and page permissions from the Chrono backend.

Chrono connects to the Chrono backend, uses protected login and stores the session token locally on the device. The existing website remains available as an independent web app.
